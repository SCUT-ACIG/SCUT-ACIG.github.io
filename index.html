About Us

Welcome to the Affective and Cognitive Intelligence Group (ACIG) at South China University of Technology. Our research focuses on multimodal sentiment analysis, virtual reality interaction, functional near-infrared spectroscopy (fNIRS), and style transfer.

We are committed to advancing the state-of-the-art in affective computing and cognitive intelligence research. Our team consists of dedicated researchers and students working on cutting-edge projects that address interdisciplinary challenges and regional industry needs.

REFN: A Multimodal Database for Emotion Analysis Using Functional Near-Infrared Spectroscopy

Dengjun Sun, Chunmei Qing*, Zhili Lai, Wanxiang Luo, Xiangmin Xu

CSIG Conference on Emotional Intelligence 2024

This paper presents REFN, a comprehensive multimodal database designed for emotion analysis research using functional near-infrared spectroscopy (fNIRS). The database integrates fNIRS signals with multiple modalities to facilitate advanced emotion recognition studies and brain-computer interface applications.
