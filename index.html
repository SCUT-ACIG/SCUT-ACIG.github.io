About Us

Welcome to the Advanced Computing and Intelligence Group (ACIG) at South China University of Technology. Our research focuses on artificial intelligence, computer vision, and deep learning.

We are committed to advancing the state-of-the-art in AI research and developing innovative solutions for real-world problems. Our team consists of dedicated researchers and students working on cutting-edge projects in machine learning and computer vision.
